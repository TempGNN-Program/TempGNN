[Evaluation Policy]
All FPGA-based designs, including TempGNN and the hardware baselines,
are synthesized, placed, and routed on the same Xilinx Alveo U280 FPGA
platform using the same Xilinx Vitis 2019.2 toolchain.

For MATG and ViTeGNN, offline model-side adaptation costs, including
model simplification, neighbor pruning, knowledge-distillation training,
and inference-mode selection, are excluded from the reported inference
latency. For all FPGA-based designs, only end-to-end inference latency
measured on the FPGA board under the corresponding deployed
model/configuration is reported.

Unless otherwise noted, all implementations use 32-bit floating-point
precision.
